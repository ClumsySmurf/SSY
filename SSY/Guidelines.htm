• When building your yoga sequences it is most often recommended to order the poses in this way 
    • Neutral or calming poses
    • Energizing poses
    • Calming poses (most important)
  • You could conceivably begin with energizing poses, but it’s important to end yoga with at least a calming pose or two to prevent the feeling of agitation when finished.  
  • Savasana is most often recommended as the last pose.  Use your best judgment to decide if Savasana is appropriate to end each particular sequence you build to meet your specific needs each time.  
  • Namaste is a greeting traditionally used to end each yoga session.  We've left Namaste off the end of the SSY Sequences so you could decide if you want to add it.
