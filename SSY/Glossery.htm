Sing Song Yogaª App

Glossary and Notes

 

pose - yoga pose, gentle stretching movement or posture

sequence - a series of yoga poses, could be thought of as an exercise routine or workout

sequence canvas - the place within the SSY App to which the user drags the poses to build a sequence (or to customize and play a SSY Sequence)

Pre-Programmed Sing Song Yogaª Sequences (SSY Sequences) - sequences put together by Sing Song Yogaª that each have a specific intended goal or purpose for the user.  Each name intends to highlight the purpose of each sequence.

sequence package - a collection of sequences bundled for better pricing.  Each package unlocks all the poses in the App.

 

 

NOTES:

 

● Some of the SSY Sequences have the same name but vary in length.  The actual length is designated at the bottom of each SSY Sequence and the relative length (long medium or short) is listed in the ÒSing Song Yogaª SequencesÓ under the Information Button.

 

● Poses have different effects on the body, or nervous system.  The poses are color coded as follows:

 

            red = energizing pose

            blue = calming pose

            yellow = neutral pose
